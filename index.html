Welcome To The Viet Kitchen

Food for Hanoians has never been a "shabby piece" - on the contrary, it is a combination of the quintessence of culture, history and lifestyle. Dong Kinh flavored rice tray (the name of Hanoi during the French colonial period), also known as a "standard North" meal tray.

When talking about the "North standard" meal tray, we all think of familiar dishes: fish stock, fresh crab soup, or fried tofu soaked with onions. Dong Kinh rice tray brings its own characteristics. The dishes are starting to be focused more on the presentation, not simply as the hard times. The ingredients started to be more varied. The ways of cooking began to be richer, adjusted for more sophistication, under the influence of French cuisine.
